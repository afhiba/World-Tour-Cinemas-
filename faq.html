Frequently Asked

Where can I get movie tickters? — new question!
Movie tickets can be purchased either at the ticket booths in person or online through our website. [answered]
Can I select my own seats?
If you are a registered member, you have the option to select your seats based on your membership status. [answered]
Can I get refills on my snacks and beverages?
We only offer refiils for popcorn and standard* beverages. [answered]
Are teenagers allowed alone at all times?
Children under the age of 18 must be accomponied by an adult after 6pm. [answered]
Can I get a refund?
If a ticket has been punched, there are no refunds, but if it was not used, a refund can be issued over the counter at least 24 hours before the showing time. [answered]
How many times can I get refills?
Great news! Refills are unlimited. [answered]

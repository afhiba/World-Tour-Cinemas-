Win Free Tickets


Join the fun at World Tour Cinemas, where there is more than just movies that awaits you. Explore our scavenger hunt times for a chance to win free tickets.

The scavenger hunt is quite simple. On Mondays and Sundays, World Tour Cinemas will put out vouchers under random seats throughout the show rooms. You will receive a piece of paper upon entry, with hints to where these seats with the vouchers may be. When you find a voucher, hold onto it. You will have the chance to redeem it for any movie of your liking, anytime.
